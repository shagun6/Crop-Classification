About The Project

In today's time, farmers face a hard time in choosing which crops to grow. Our main motive of the project is to use emerging deep learning concepts to help farmers to gain maximum profit and also boost their prouction. Our website just requires a pic of the crop and through one pic farmer will get all the important information of that particular crop.

In today's time, farmers face a hard time in choosing which crops to grow. Our website just requires a pic of the crop and through one pic farmer will get all the important information of that particular crop. Through this, they can just have to upload pic of a crop(no matter even if they don't know which type of crop is that), our website will predict the crop type and also help the farmers to know different factors like temperature, climate, soil suitable for that particular crop, market price of the crop and states in which that crop is mostly grown. This will evetually help them to plan their sowing accordingly.

We basically used deep learning concepts to train our model to predict crop type. We have used Python Web framework Flask and integrate deep learning models with it. We used a dataset containig 804 images to train our model and used dataset of 201 images to test the model.

We are basically predicting 5 types of crops.
  • Jute
  • Maize
  • Sugarcane
  • Rice
  • Wheat

In future, we can also add the feature in our website that will also tell the geolocation of the image uploaded along with the type of the crop. We can take more crops for classification and also we can take larger image dataset which will eventually increase prediction accuracy.
